Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A simple brochure website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex e-commerce platform could take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeline based on your requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based and hourly pricing models. Project-based pricing is ideal for clients with clearly defined requirements, while hourly rates work well for ongoing development or projects with evolving needs. You can view our standard packages on our pricing page, or contact us for a custom quote.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our maintenance plans ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally.

Can you help with website content creation?

Absolutely! We offer content creation services, including copywriting, image selection, and graphic design. Our team can help craft compelling content that engages your audience and aligns with your brand voice.

Do you provide hosting for websites?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions with various packages to suit different needs. Our hosting services include regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support to ensure your website remains accessible and secure.

What is your approach to website security?

Security is a top priority in all our projects. We implement best practices such as secure coding standards, regular updates, SSL certificates, firewall protection, and secure authentication methods. We also conduct security audits and stay updated on the latest security threats.
