Meet the EnnieDevelopers CEO

Enson Mndolo is a highly skilled professional with a strong academic background in both Technical Education Technology and Information and Communication Technology (ICT). He holds a Bachelor's degree in Technical Education Technology from Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS) and a Diploma in Information and Communication from SOS Vocational Training Center.

Enson combines his educational and practical expertise to approach technology solutions across various sectors. He is passionate about applying technology in fields such as education, business, churches, and organizations. His goal is to help individuals and institutions leverage modern technology to improve efficiency, drive innovation, and achieve their objectives. Whether enhancing communication, optimizing processes, or contributing to growth, Enson consistently makes a positive impact in every environment he engages with.

With over 5 years of experience in the industry, Enson and his team combines technical expertise with creative design to deliver websites that not only look stunning but also perform exceptionally well.
