Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our pricing plans

What's included in the website setup fee?

The website setup fee includes the initial design and development of your website based on the plan you choose. This covers responsive design, basic SEO optimization, integration with your social media accounts, and all the features listed in your selected plan.

Do I need to pay for website hosting separately?

Website hosting is not included in the base pricing plans, but we offer reliable hosting packages starting at $99/month that include regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support. You can also choose to host your website with another provider if you prefer.

Can I upgrade my plan in the future?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time as your business grows and your needs change. We'll calculate the upgrade cost based on the work required to add the new features and functionality to your existing website.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your website and the plan you choose. Generally, a Basic website takes 2-4 weeks, a Professional website takes 4-6 weeks, and an Enterprise website takes 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a more specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for all our services. Typically, we require a 20% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion and your satisifaction. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. Please contact us to discuss payment options that work for you.

What if I need a feature not listed in any plan?

We offer custom development services for any specific functionality you might need. During our consultation, we'll discuss your unique requirements and provide a custom quote for any additional features not included in our standard plans.

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

We're confident you'll love your new website. If you're not completely satisfied with the final result, we'll revise it until you are. We offer unlimited revisions during the development phase and a 30-day post-launch support period to ensure everything works perfectly.
